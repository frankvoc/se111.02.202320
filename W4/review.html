Review of Yellowstone

Yellowstone is a TV show packed with drama and action. It is entirely filmed in Yellowstone National Park, which allows for some beautiful cinematography as seen in the show. It is not the typical Cowboy show/movie but rather one with a modern twist. With John and family having to deal with the changing environment around his ranch, causing them to protect their ranch by any means. This combined with the deep character development will have any viewer incredibly immersed in this show.

Frank Vocatura

February 8, 2023


★★★★☆

Yellowstone Trivia

  • The director; Taylor Sheridan makes many appearences in the show as a very wealthy Horse Trader.
  • Cole Hauser who plays Rip Wheeler did not need an audition after Taylor Sheridan met him.
  • Kevin Costner who plays John Dutton has a band called "Kevin Coster & Modern West" who wrote songs for the show.
